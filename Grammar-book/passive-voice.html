The Passive Voice

Shifting the Focus to the Action

📘 1. Understanding the Rule

The passive voice is formed with be + past participle. The object of the active sentence becomes the subject; the original subject can be added with "by", or dropped entirely.

TenseActivePassive
Present simpleThey make cars here.Cars are made here.
Past simpleThey built this in 1990.This was built in 1990.
Present perfectThey have cancelled it.It has been cancelled.
Future / modalThey will announce it.It will be announced.
👤 Doer unknown/unimportant
My bike was stolen.
🎓 Formal/scientific writing
The data was analysed.
🔎 Focus on the action, not the agent
The building is being renovated.
🖊️ Mentioning the agent when relevant
The novel was written by her.
