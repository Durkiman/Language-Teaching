📘 1. Understanding the Skill

A scaffold is a set of ready-made phrases for a real-world situation, so you don't have to build sentences from scratch under pressure. Three common scenario families:

☕ Café / ordering
"Could I get...", "For here or to go?"
💼 Business
"I wanted to follow up on...", "Could we schedule a call?"
✈️ Travel
"Where's the nearest...?", "Could I see your boarding pass?"
Politeness matters: "Could I..." and "Would it be possible to..." sound more natural than direct commands ("Give me...") in almost every real-world scenario.
