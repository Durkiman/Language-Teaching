Personal Information 🧳

A1-02
Exchanging personal information
Can-do: Can ask for and give personal information: address, nationality, age, family, job, hobbies.
Functions: giving personal information
Grammar: questions · present simple, 1st/2nd person
Lexis: nationalities and countries · personal information
Evidence: Ask five personal questions and answer five, including one about family and one about work/hobbies; complete a hotel check-in or landing-card form.

Question and answer, side by side. Question et réponse, côte à côte.

"What's your name?"
"My name is Angelique."
Comment vous appelez-vous ? — Je m'appelle Angelique.
"Where are you from?"
"I'm from Belgium."
D'où venez-vous ? — Je viens de Belgique.
"What's your nationality?"
"I'm Belgian."
Quelle est votre nationalité ? — Je suis belge.
"How old are you?"
"I'm fifty-three years old."
Quel âge avez-vous ? — J'ai cinquante-trois ans.
"Where do you live?"
"I live in Wallonia."
Où habitez-vous ? — J'habite en Wallonie.
"What's your job?"
"I'm a teacher." / "I teach yoga."
Quel est votre métier ? — Je suis professeure. / J'enseigne le yoga.
"Do you have children?"
"Yes, I have two children." / "No, I don't."
Avez-vous des enfants ? — Oui, j'ai deux enfants. / Non, je n'en ai pas.
"What are your hobbies?"
"I like nature and art."
Quels sont vos passe-temps ? — J'aime la nature et l'art.
