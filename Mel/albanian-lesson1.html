Part 3 of 4 · Grammar

Emrat e shquar
Definite nouns — how it works

In Albanian, the definite article goes at the end of the noun — not in front like in French or English.
🇫🇷 French: le pain → Albanian: buka (bread + a)
Masculine nouns add -i (or -u after k, g, h)
Feminine nouns add -a (or -ja)
Think of it like: noun + article fused together
