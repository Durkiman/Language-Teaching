Çka
don?

What do you want? Today you speak before you're ready — and that's exactly the point.

The structure
Du / Don / Doni + me + verb
Du me hangër. — I want to eat.
Don me shku? — Do you want to go?
S'du me fol tash. — I don't want to talk now.
A don kafe? — Do you want coffee?
Tap to explore — key words
Du
I want
Je veux
Du me hangër bukë. — I want to eat bread.
S'du tash. — I don't want to (now).
Don
You want / He/She wants
Tu veux / Il·elle veut
A don kafe? — Do you want coffee?
Don me shku? — Do you want to go?
Dumë
We want
Nous voulons
Dumë me shku bashkë. — We want to go together.
S'du / Nuk du
I don't want
Je ne veux pas
S'du me hangër tash. — I don't want to eat now.
Jo, s'du. — No, I don't want to.
me + verb
infinitive marker
équivalent de "de + infinitif"
me hangër — to eat
me shku — to go
me fol — to talk
me pi — to drink
A don...?
Do you want...?
Tu veux... ? / Veux-tu... ?
A don kafe ose çaj? — Do you want coffee or tea?
A don me vij? — Do you want to come?
