⚙️ Grammar — i vs e adjectives
Adjectives agree with gender. Mel always uses e — Ndihem e lumtur, jam e sigurt. Durim uses i — ndihem i lumtur. Same as heureuse / heureux in French.

Yes/No questions — with A
The "A" marker
Put A at the start of a sentence to make it a yes/no question — no word order change needed.

Po punon. → He is working.
A po punon? → Is he working?

🇫🇷 Think of "A" like "Est-ce que" — it's a question marker, not a word that changes the rest.

💬 Bounce-back roleplay
0:00
The skill

Bounce-back means: after you respond, you immediately throw a question or reaction back. It's what turns a monologue into a conversation. Mel's goal is to never let a silence be the last word.
