Free Production · 15 min

Now it's your turn

Use today's phrases to talk about yourself. Your teacher will listen — don't worry about mistakes, just speak!

Your Speaking Prompts
1
Tell me your name, where you're from, and how old you are.
Use: "My name is… / I'm from… / I'm … years old."
2
Describe what you do professionally in 2–3 sentences.
Use: "I work as… / I help people… / My clients…"
3
What are you passionate about? Why did you choose this work?
Use: "I'm passionate about… / I believe that…"
4
Talk about your goals and dreams for the future.
Use: "My goal is to… / I dream of… / One day, I want to…"
5
What do you do in your free time?
Use: "In my free time, I… / I love… / I enjoy…"
