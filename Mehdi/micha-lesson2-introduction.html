Key Phrases

My name is…
"My name is Micha."
Je m'appelle…
First thing to say in any intro
I'm … years old
"I'm 12 years old."
J'ai … ans
NOT "I have 12 years" ⚠️
I come from…
"I come from France."
Je viens de…
Also: "I'm from…"
I love / I like…
"I love playing Brawl Stars."
J'adore / J'aime…
+ verb-ing after love/like
In my free time…
"In my free time I play games."
Dans mon temps libre…
Great for talking about hobbies
My favourite… is…
"My favourite brawler is Spike."
Mon préféré… c'est…
favourite = préféré(e)
One day I want to…
"One day I want to be a pro player."
Un jour je veux…
Talking about dreams & goals
Nice to meet you!
Said at the end of an introduction.
Enchanté(e) !
