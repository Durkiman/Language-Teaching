Chapter 2

Comparatives & Superlatives

Two tools for comparing things — one for 2 items, one for the most extreme.

Deux outils pour comparer — "plus ___ que" et "le/la plus ___".
The Three Forms
Base
🇫🇷 La forme normale
Just the adjective.
big · tall · beautiful
"The Azores is a volcanic island."
Comparative
🇫🇷 Compare 2 choses — "plus ___ que"
2 things. Always "than" after!
bigger than · more beautiful than
"The Azores is greener than Belgium."
Superlative
🇫🇷 Le plus extrême — "le/la plus ___"
Most extreme. Always "the" before!
the biggest · the most beautiful
"It is the most remote island."
How to Form Them
TypeBaseComparativeSuperlative🇫🇷 Rule
Short (1 syllable)big · tall · hot · deepbigger · taller · hotter · deeperthe biggest · the hottestAdd -er / -est
CVC → double last letter
big→bigger · hot→hotter
Ending -ypretty · rockyprettier · rockierthe prettiestChange -y → -ier / -iest
Long (2+ syllables)beautiful · volcanic · remotemore beautiful · more volcanicthe most beautifulAdd more / the most before
Irregular ⚠️good · bad · farbetter · worse · furtherthe best · the worst · the furthestMust memorise!
good → better → the best
3 Most Common Mistakes
1.
"more bigger than Belgium" → "bigger than Belgium"🇫🇷 Jamais "more" + "-er" ensemble — un seul à la fois !
2.
"the most biggest lake" → "the biggest lake"🇫🇷 Jamais "the most" + "-est" ensemble.
3.
"bigger Belgium" → "bigger than Belgium"🇫🇷 Le comparatif a TOUJOURS besoin de "than" après !

🎤 Text A — Teacher reads · Micha listens
An Island Unlike Any Other
The Azores is one of the most isolated archipelagos on Earth. São Miguel, the largest island, sits in the Atlantic — further from Lisbon than Lisbon is from London. The landscape is greener than almost anywhere in Europe, and its volcanic lakes are deeper than any lake in mainland Portugal. The most famous of these is Sete Cidades — two differently coloured lakes inside the same crater.

In Furnas Valley, the ground is hotter than you would expect — locals cook food directly in the volcanic soil, a tradition older than the oldest restaurants in Lisbon. Durim is here right now, on ground that is the most volcanically active in all of Portugal.
📖 Text B — Micha reads
The Whales and the Weather
The Atlantic around São Miguel is colder than the Mediterranean, but home to some of the most spectacular marine life in the world. Sperm whales live here all year. They are bigger than any land animal — their dives are deeper than most submarines can go.

The weather is more unpredictable than in Belgium — rain and sunshine in the same morning. But the result is an island wilder than California, more volcanic than Samoa, and quieter than Saint-Barths. It might be the most surprising place in all of Durim's lessons.
