Grammar — Going To

Talking about the future 🚀

We use going to when we have a plan or intention. You already decided! 🇫🇷 On l'utilise pour les projets et intentions — tu as déjà décidé !

Subject
I / She / They…
🇫🇷 Sujet
am/is/are + going to
am going to
is going to
are going to
🇫🇷 aller + infinitif
Base Verb
play / eat / win…
🇫🇷 Infinitif

All forms 🇫🇷

PronounFormExample🇫🇷 French
Iam going toI am going to play.Je vais jouer.
Youare going toYou are going to win.Tu vas gagner.
He / She / Itis going toShe is going to eat.Elle va manger.
Weare going toWe are going to travel.Nous allons voyager.
Theyare going toThey are going to run.Ils vont courir.

Negative & Questions 🇫🇷

TypeStructureExample🇫🇷 French
❌ Negativeam/is/are + not going toI am not going to lose.Je ne vais pas perdre.
❓ QuestionAm/Is/Are + subject + going to?Are you going to come?Tu vas venir ?
✅ Short answerYes, I am. / No, I'm not.Are you going to play? Yes, I am!Oui, je vais le faire ! / Non.

⚠️ Common Mistakes — Erreurs fréquentes

I going to eat. → I am going to eat. 🇫🇷 Oublie pas le verbe be !
She is going to played. → She is going to play. 🇫🇷 Après going to : infinitif, pas past tense !
They are go to win. → They are going to win. 🇫🇷 C'est going to, pas juste go to !

Exercises

Practice time 💪

Easy
1. Complete: She _______ going to travel tomorrow.
🇫🇷 Hint 💡 Grammar
🇫🇷 Elle va voyager demain. — she → quelle forme de be ?
💡 He / She / It → is
Easy
2. Complete: They _______ going to win the tournament.
🇫🇷 Hint 💡 Grammar
🇫🇷 Ils vont gagner le tournoi. — they → ?
💡 We / You / They → are
Medium
3. Write the full sentence: I / not / going to / lose
🇫🇷 Hint 💡 Grammar
🇫🇷 Je ne vais pas perdre.
💡 Négatif : am/is/are + not + going to + base verb
Medium
4. Which sentence is correct?
🇫🇷 Hint 💡 Grammar
🇫🇷 Il va manger une pizza. — Besoin du verbe be + going to complet !
💡 He → is going to + base verb
Hard
5. Make a question: _______ she going to cook tonight?
🇫🇷 Hint 💡 Grammar
🇫🇷 Est-ce qu'elle va cuisiner ce soir ? — she → quelle forme de be ?
💡 Question : Is/Are/Am + subject + going to?
she → Is
Hard
6. Translate to English: Nous allons commencer demain.
🇫🇷 Hint 💡 Grammar
🇫🇷 nous → we · allons commencer → are going to start
💡 We → are going to + base verb
