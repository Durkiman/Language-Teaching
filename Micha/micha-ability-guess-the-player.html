📚 Sentence Bank — use these to describe

Banque de phrases — utilise-les pour décrire

He plays for ___.Il joue pour ___.
He is a ___ (striker / defender / goalkeeper...).Il est ___ (attaquant / défenseur / gardien...).
He has got ___ hair (short / long / curly...).Il a les cheveux ___ (courts / longs / bouclés...).
He has got a beard. / He hasn't got a beard.Il a une barbe. / Il n'a pas de barbe.
He wears number ___.Il porte le numéro ___.
He comes from ___ (country).Il vient de ___ (pays).
He is very tall / quite short.Il est très grand / assez petit.

💪 Talking about ability — can / can't

Parler de ce qu'un joueur sait faire — can / can't

He can score with both feet.Il peut marquer des deux pieds.
He can run very fast.Il peut courir très vite.
He can head the ball well.Il peut bien jouer de la tête.
He can't play well with his head. / He can't head the ball well.Il ne peut pas bien jouer de la tête.
He can dribble past defenders.Il peut dribbler les défenseurs.

🎯 Worked example (to model the pattern for Micha)

"He plays for Manchester City. He is a striker. He is very tall — he has got a strong, powerful body. He can score with both feet, but he can't head the ball as well as you'd expect. He hasn't got long hair. He comes from Norway. He wears number 9."

Tour 2 — Micha décrit, tu devines
