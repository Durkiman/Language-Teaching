🧠 Retrieval — Possessives + Saba
1
Give the possessive pronoun for: I · she · we · they
Donne le pronom possessif pour : je → ? · elle → ? · nous → ? · ils → ?
I → mine (le mien)  ·  she → hers (le sien)  ·  we → ours (le nôtre)  ·  they → theirs (le leur)
2
Fix the mistake: "This is theirs trophy."
Corrige l'erreur : "theirs" ne peut pas précéder un nom.
❌ "This is theirs trophy." → ✅ "This is their trophy." — "their" est l'adjectif possessif, "theirs" est le pronom (sans nom après).
01 Read — Tonight's Final 10 MIN
Match Preview
Paris Saint-Germain and Arsenal Clash in Budapest for European Glory
By Our Football Correspondent · Budapest, 30 May 2026

The most prestigious club competition in world football reaches its climax tonight at the Puskás Aréna in Budapest, as Arsenal and Paris Saint-Germain contest the 2026 UEFA Champions League Final.

For Arsenal, this is a moment that has been 20 years in the making. The Gunners last reached a Champions League final in 2006, where they lost to Barcelona in Paris. They arrive in Hungary having just won the Premier League title for the first time in 22 years — making this potentially the most remarkable double in the club's history.

À retenir : "to reach" = atteindre · "a moment in the making" = un moment qui se prépare depuis longtemps · "the Gunners" = surnom d'Arsenal (les artilleurs)

Paris Saint-Germain enter as the defending champions. Last season, they defeated Arsenal in the semi-finals before dismantling Inter Milan 5-0 in the final in Munich. Tonight, they chase history — if they win, they will become only the second club ever to defend the Champions League title in the modern era, after Real Madrid's famous three-in-a-row between 2016 and 2018.

PSG's key danger man is Khvicha Kvaratskhelia, the Georgian winger who has been the tournament's most devastating attacker. He equalled Zlatan Ibrahimović's PSG record for most goals in a single Champions League campaign. Alongside him, Ousmane Dembélé — the Ballon d'Or winner — returns from a calf injury to start the final.

À retenir : "defending champions" = champions en titre · "to chase history" = courir après l'histoire · "danger man" = joueur le plus dangereux · "winger" = ailier

For Arsenal, the defensive trio of David Raya, William Saliba, and Gabriel have been exceptional all season, conceding the fewest goals in both the Premier League and this Champions League campaign. Captain Martin Ødegaard carries the hopes of an entire fanbase as he attempts to become the first Arsenal captain ever to lift the Champions League trophy.

One fascinating subplot: this final revisits last season's semi-final, where PSG knocked Arsenal out. Tonight, the Gunners have a chance for revenge. With Jurriën Timber returning from injury and the electric Bukayo Saka in form, Arsenal have the quality to cause PSG serious problems.

03 Football Positions — On the Pitch 10 MIN
The Football Pitch — Les Positions
🧤
GK Goalkeeper
🇫🇷 Gardien de but
Protects the goal. The only player who can use their hands.
🛡️
CB Centre-Back
🇫🇷 Défenseur central
Stops the opposition's strikers. Must be strong and tall.
⬅️
LB Left-Back
🇫🇷 Arrière gauche
Defends the left side. Often attacks too — overlapping runs.
➡️
RB Right-Back
🇫🇷 Arrière droit
Defends the right side. Makes overlapping runs to support attack.
🧱
DM Defensive Midfielder
🇫🇷 Milieu défensif / Sentinelle
Sits in front of the defence. Breaks up attacks, wins the ball back.
⚙️
CM Central Midfielder
🇫🇷 Milieu central
Controls the tempo of the game. Passes, runs, defends and attacks.
🔟
CAM Attacking Midfielder
🇫🇷 Meneur de jeu / Numéro 10
Creates chances for strikers. The most creative player. The "number 10".
⚡
LW / RW Winger
🇫🇷 Ailier gauche / Ailier droit
Attacks from the sides. Fast and direct. Crosses or cuts inside to shoot.
🎯
ST Striker / Centre-Forward
🇫🇷 Avant-centre / Attaquant de pointe
The goalscorer. Always in the box, always looking to finish.
🔄
SS Second Striker / False 9
🇫🇷 Deuxième attaquant / Faux 9
Drops deep to create, not just score. Confuses defenders.
