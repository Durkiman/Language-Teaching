Part 2 🧭 The Compass

🌍 Why does it work?

The Earth is like a giant magnet. The compass needle is also magnetic, so it always points toward the North Pole — no matter where you are!

🏴‍☠️ The Log Pose

On the Grand Line, normal compasses don't work because of magnetic islands. That's why Nami uses a Log Pose — it locks onto island magnetic fields instead!

Luffy is the captain of the Straw Hat Pirates. He is a young man with short black hair, a red vest, and his famous straw hat — a gift from his hero, Shanks.

He ate the Gum-Gum Devil Fruit as a child, which made his entire body stretch like rubber. Because of this, he became unable to swim — a serious problem for a pirate!

Luffy is incredibly brave, sometimes reckless, and always loyal to his friends. He never gave up on anyone, no matter how dangerous the enemy was. His dream is to find the One Piece and become King of the Pirates.

🗡️ Roronoa Zoro

Role

Zoro is the first mate and the strongest fighter on the crew. He has short green hair, a muscular body, and three earrings in his left ear. He always carried three swords — one in each hand and one in his mouth!

He trained as a swordsman since he was a child. He made a promise to his childhood friend Kuina that he would become the greatest swordsman in the world. He never forgot that promise.

Zoro is serious, determined, and disciplined — but he has one famous weakness: he cannot read maps and always got lost, even on a straight road!
