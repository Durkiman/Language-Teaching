01 Past Simple — Match Report Grammar 8 MIN
📐 How to write about a match — Past Simple reference 🇫🇷 WITH FRENCH
Type Formula Football Example 🇫🇷 French
POSITIVE
Subject + past verb
Regular: add -ed   Irregular: change form
Havertz scored in the 6th minute.
PSG won on penalties.
Havertz a marqué à la 6e minute.
Le PSG a gagné aux penalties.
NEGATIVE
Subject + didn't + base verb
Always use BASE form after "didn't"
Arsenal didn't win the trophy.
Gabriel didn't score his penalty.
Arsenal n'a pas gagné le trophée.
Gabriel n'a pas marqué son penalty.
QUESTION
Did + subject + base verb?
Always use BASE form after "Did"
Did Arsenal score first?
Did Saka play well?
Est-ce qu'Arsenal a marqué en premier ?
Est-ce que Saka a bien joué ?
⚡ The KEY rule: After didn't and Did…? — always use the BASE form of the verb (not past tense).
❌ "He didn't scored" → ✅ "He didn't score"    ❌ "Did he won?" → ✅ "Did he win?"
🇫🇷 Après "didn't" et "Did…?" on utilise TOUJOURS l'infinitif (la forme de base) — jamais le passé !
