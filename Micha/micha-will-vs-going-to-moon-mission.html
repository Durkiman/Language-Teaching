📐 "Going to" — Predictions & Plans

Prédictions et plans

subject + am/is/are + going to + verb
SubjectFormExample
Iam going toI am going to watch the launch.
You / We / Theyare going toThey are going to fly around the Moon.
He / She / Itis going toShe is going to be the pilot.

Je vais... / Ils vont... / Elle va...

Negative

subject + am/is/are + not + going to + verb

e.g. "They are not going to land on the Moon."

Ils ne vont pas atterrir sur la Lune.

⚠️ Common mistake: Don't forget "to"! Not "going land" → "going to land".

N'oublie pas "to" !

📝 Reading — Mission Briefing

Lecture — Briefing de mission

Next year, four astronauts are going to fly around the Moon on Artemis II. They are not going to land — they are going to test the spacecraft first. Last time, in the Apollo missions, astronauts went to the Moon and walked on it. This time is different. The crew is going to spend ten days in space and then is going to come back safely to Earth.

Easy

1. Fill in the blank: The astronauts ___ ___ fly around the Moon.

💡 Grammar tip
Use "are going to" because the subject is plural (astronauts = they).
🇫🇷 French hint
"sont en train d'aller" → are going to
Easy

2. Choose the correct form: Christina ___ the Mission Specialist.

is going to be
are going to be
is going be
🇫🇷 French hint
Christina = elle → "is going to"
Medium

3. Make it negative: "They are going to land on the Moon."

💡 Grammar tip
Add "not" right after "are": are + not + going to
🇫🇷 French hint
Ils ne vont pas atterrir sur la Lune.
Medium

4. Past or future? "Last year, astronauts went to the ISS."

Past Simple
Going to future
🇫🇷 French hint
"went" = forme passée de "go" → c'est le passé !
Hard

5. Fill in both blanks: Last time, astronauts ___ (walk) on the Moon. This time, they ___ ___ just fly around it.

 
💡 Grammar tip
First blank = Past Simple (it happened). Second blank = going to (it's planned).
Hard

6. Predict! What do you think is going to happen on Artemis III? Write one full sentence.

🇫🇷 French hint
Je pense que les astronautes vont...

📝 Teacher-Read Fallback Script

Script de secours — lis à voix haute, mots-clés en gras

"Our Artemis II Moon mission is complete! The Space Launch System rocket launched the crew into space. The Orion spacecraft gave the astronauts a safe home away from home. They flew around the Moon, observed its far side, and did science. It was a new human spaceflight distance record. The crew safely returned to Earth. It inspired the world!"

🧠 Listen & Check

Écoute et vérifie

1. Did the astronauts land on the Moon during Artemis II?

Les astronautes ont-ils atterri sur la Lune pendant Artemis II ?

No — they flew around the Moon but did not land. That comes with Artemis III!

2. What record did the crew set?

Quel record l'équipage a-t-il établi ?

A new human spaceflight distance record!

3. Was this past simple or going-to future in the video? Why?

C'était le passé simple ou le futur "going to" dans la vidéo ? Pourquoi ?

Past Simple — because the mission already happened, it's not a prediction anymore!
